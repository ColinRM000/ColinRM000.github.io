RustPal FAQ

Why does RustPal go offline?

If RustPal ever goes offline, it is more than likely due to updates.

What language was RustPal developed with?

RustPal is written in discord.py (Python)

How can I become a support member?

To join the RustPal Team in the support role, you will need to create a ticket in #support in our Discord server.

Will there be more features in the future?

We are currently working on adding more features for a paid version of RustPal which will cost $2.99 a month or $15.00 for lifetime. More information will be announced on this in the future.
